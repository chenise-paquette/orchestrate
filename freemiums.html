How do you download music on Orchestrate?

Not all songs are downloadable. If you don't see a ‘download’ button, it means the person who posted it did not intend for it to be downloadable.

What are Playlists and how do I make them?

To create a playlist, click on ‘Add to playlist’ below any sound- your own or someone else's- to place it into a playlist.

What can I do if I feel offended on Orchestrate?

We would advise blocking anyone you don’t want to interact with and avoiding going to their profile.

Does Orchestrate offer customer support?

Yes, we have FAQ help page and a help forum made by other users.

How do I get more followers and plays on Orchestrate?

Adding your tracks to groups, following people whose music you enjoy, and leaving genuine comments on their tracks can promote yourself within the Orchestrate community.

Can I dowload the recording application on to my computer?

It is only available as an application if you get the premium account. You are able to use it wirelessly on the pro account.

How do I find interesting people I can follow?

The easiest way is for you to browse from one member to another, check out some of their creations and follow the ones you like.

Can I find radio streaming on Orchestrate?

You can search for radio program by searching for specific shows, genres, and tags in the search bar.

What types of files can I upload?

We take AIFF, WAVE (WAV), FLAC, ALAC, OGG, MP2, MP3, AAC, AMR, and WMA files.
